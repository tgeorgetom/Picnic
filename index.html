How does Picnic work

01

POOLED PREMIUMS

When you join the collective, your premium is added to a pool of funds drawn from all members.

02

COVER CLAIMS

The pool pays for claims and costs, with a small reserve secured.

03

04

PROFITS RETURNED

Remaining profits are returned to you through lower premiums.

Share in the Picnic

Security Tokens are similar to preference shares in a company. The holder of a Security Token has ownership rights in Picnic. Among other things, they provide the owner a share of assets on liquidation, and early backers can trade them on the market when their value changes.

By participating in the Picnic Security Token Offer, you're not just buying an asset that has the potential to add to your wealth - you're helping to transform the insurance industry and lowering the costs to protect your family and others around the world.
