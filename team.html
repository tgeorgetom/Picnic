Why we want to change insurance

We believe that insurance exists to protect you at your most vulnerable. A rescue from the unforseen and unavoidable problems that you might not survive alone, but a collective helping each other can weather.

That's why we're restoring the insurance mutual with technology. To allow communities, not a corporation to provide coverage with compassion. To maximise protection, instead of maximising profit. To create insurance with heart.
